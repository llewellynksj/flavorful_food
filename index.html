Chicken & Black Bean Enchiladas

A Flavour Explosion!

A perfect mixture of spices, tender meat,
brightly coloured veg, and delicious black beans

...all wrapped up in a perfect tortilla parcel!

It's all about that sauce!

A harmonious balance of spicy, smoky, sweet, and savory notes; the sauce, made from a carefully crafted blend of ingredients, is the star of the show, coating every bite with its complex, deeply satisfying taste.

The cheese adds richness, and the meat, whether it's succulent chicken, tender beef, or flavorful pork, provides a hearty and satisfying element.

Ingredients

Serves 4

Sauce:

  • 2 x 400g tinned tomatoes
  • 1 x white onion, diced
  • 3 x garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 x tbsp chilli powder
  • 2 x tsp cumin
  • 2 x tbsp oregano
  • 2 x tbsp lime or lemon juice
  • 1 x tsp fresh rosemary, chopped (optional)
  • 1 x pinch onion powder (optional)
  • 1 x pinch paprika (optional)

Enchiladas:

  • 6-8 flour/corn tortillas
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • 2 x green peppers, sliced
  • 2 x red peppers, sliced
  • 1 x large white onion, sliced
  • 600g (approx) chicken breast, diced thinly
  • 1 x green chilli, finely diced
  • 1 x tsp paprika
  • 1 x 400g can of black beans
  • 250g grated cheese
  • Fresh coriander to garnish

Recipe

To make the sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over a medium heat. Add the onion and brown off (approx. 6-8 mins). Add the minced garlic and cook for a further minute.
  2. While the onion is cooking add all the other sauce ingredients to a blender (you may need to do this in 2 batches).
  3. Once cooked, add the onion and garlic mix to the blender, and blend until smooth.
  4. Wipe down the skillet getting rid of any leftover pieces of onion.
  5. Add the blended sauce to the skillet and simmer gently for 10-15 mins. You can do this 10 mins before you're ready to construct the enchiladas.

Try the sauce and add more chilli to taste

Recipe

Make the Enchilada mixture:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 190° (Fan oven)
  2. Heat oil in two separate pans over a medium heat. In one pan start to cook the onions and peppers off. In the other pan start to brown the chicken (you may need to do this in batches).
  3. Once the chicken is cooked through add to the onion/pepper mix.
  4. Add a pinch of paprika and the diced chilli to the onions and peppers after around 6-8 mins of cooking.
  5. Heat your black beans on the hob or in the microwave, drain.

Assemble:

  1. Put a spoonful of sauce on the base of a tortilla and spread across it.
  2. Put a spoon of black beans in a line down the middle of the tortilla.
  3. Spread the chicken/onion/pepper mixture in a line down the middle of the tortilla.
  4. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  5. Roll the tortilla, folding in the ends.
  6. Place in an oven proof roasting dish.
  7. Repeat until all the mixture/tortillas are used up.
  8. Pour the remaining sauce over the enchiladas and sprinkle the remaining cheese over the top.
  9. Bake in the oven for 20-30 mins or until cheese is melted and golden.
  10. Garnish with the coriander before serving.
